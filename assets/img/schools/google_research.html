Google 2022 PhD Fellowships

  • Share This: Share on TwitterShare on LinkedinShare on Facebook
August 15, 2021

Google PhD Fellowship students are a select group recognized by Google researchers and their institutions as some of the most promising young academics in the world. The Fellowships are awarded to students who represent the future of research in the following fields:

  • Algorithms, Optimizations and Markets
  • Computational Neural and Cognitive Sciences
  • Health Research
  • Human-Computer Interaction
  • Machine Learning
  • Machine Perception, Speech Technology and Computer Vision
  • Mobile Computing
  • Natural Language Processing (including Information Retrieval and Extraction)
  • Privacy and Security
  • Programming Languages and Software Engineering
  • Quantum Computing
  • Recommender Systems
  • Structured Data and Database Management
  • Systems and Networking

 Fellowship Details

  • 2 year Fellowship with opportunity to extend to 3rd year.  Nominees must be expected to earn their PhD on or after June 2024.
  • Full tuition and fees (enrollment fees, health insurance, books) plus a stipend to be used for living expenses, travel and personal equipment
  • Google Research Mentor
Applications must be submitted to OGE’s Director of Graduate Fellowships, Scott Tirrell stirrell@mit.edu as a single PDF by 5:00pm on Thursday, September 9, 2021.
Find further details on eligibility and the application process here.

By Amanda Cornwall
Amanda Cornwall Associate Director, Graduate Student Professional Development
