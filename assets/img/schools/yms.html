Frequently Asked Questions:

Who are our medical doctors?

Our physicians are all Ivy-League trained from universities like Yale and the University of Southern California (USC) and are all professors of clinical medicine in their respective fields. All graduated at the top of their classes and are board certified and licensed in the United States.

What can our doctors help you with?

Sometimes it is hard to find doctors locally that have the training to help you with your medical issues or you may find yourself in a situation where you may just want a second opinion. This may be in regards to a planned intervention or surgery, or it may be discussing test or imaging results with an expert doctor. Whatever your reason, our doctors are qualified to help.

And in the off-chance that they might not know the answer or feel like they are unable to help you, your visit is free.Â 

What does the process look like?

The process is quick, simple and 100% private and confidential. It only takes a few minutes to create an account. Choose if you want to be called by the doctor or if prefer a video or chat consultation. If you have test results or photos to upload you can do this in your account using the browser. During the consultation, the doctor will ask you a few question and then give you recommendations. You may follow up with them by email for free if there are any related questions. If we cannot help you, your visit is free.

How should I prepare for the doctor's appointment?

It is a good idea to upload any possible test results to the patient folder prior to your consultation so that the doctor can review it beforehand. If you are using a desktop computer, make sure your browser has access to the camera and microphone if you are planning on connecting to us via video. For the most seamless experience we recommend downloading our free app (iOS or Android).

Will this be confidential and private?

Yes. Everything you discuss with the doctor during the consultation will be 100% confidential. Our platform is encrypted and we never sell your information to third parties for profit. If you agree to communication by email for additional information, please note that emails are not always secure.
